About Us

Dojang Limited is a sports learning experience that sets the standard for excellence in chess instruction. Integrating new Concepts in teaching chess, our curriculum reflects children's interests and builds on their competencies, we excel in creating personal bond with the students there by understanding their unique learning styles and tailor the training accordingly with personalized attention in every class.

Since 2022, Magic Square Academy has been a competitive force in the Tumkur area. We hone raw talent to maturity with quality coaching, primal workouts and a true love of the sport. Here we believe in making the learning process as enjoyable as posible.
